Research

Selected publications and research areas across computer vision, signal processing, large language models, geoscience, and healthcare.

My research focuses on efficient and structure-aware learning methods for scientific and real-world sensing problems, with recurring themes in efficiency, limited supervision, and geometry-aware modeling for deployment in ecology and healthcare.
